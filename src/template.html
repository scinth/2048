What is 2048?

2048 is a game created by an Italian developer Gabriele Cirulli way back on 2014 based on 1024 by Veewo Studio.

How to play?

Use Arrow-keys (on desktop) or swipe with your fingers up, down, left and right (on mobile) to move the tiles.

Two adjacent tiles with the same values will merge (with relevance to the direction of their movement) into a single tile containing their sum.

Your goal is to reach the value 2048!. If you win the game you can choose to continue playing to reach a higher value, the highest possible value is 131072 on a standard (4 x 4) grid.

How to win?

  1. Swipe left and right several times.
  2. Build a higher tile in a corner.
  3. Keep the row with the high tile filled.
  4. Focus on combining small tiles.
  5. Maneuver around small trapped tiles.
  6. Move your corner tile when forced to, then return it.
  7. Keep trying until you win.
